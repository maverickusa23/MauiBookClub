Welcome to the Club, Ohana!

Sharing your favorite stories shouldn't feel like rocket science. Follow this simple beach-walk guide to upload your books to our digital library using just your web browser.

Step One

Navigate to the Island Repository

Open the GitHub link shared in our newsletter. Look for the folder named books/. This is where all our shared treasures live.

lightbulb PRO-TIP

Bookmark this page in your browser so you can find your way back to the beach anytime!

upload_file
Step Two

Add Your Files

Click the "Add file" button in the top right, then choose "Upload files". You can drag and drop your book summaries or digital copies directly into the box.

history_edu
Step Three

Leave a Note (Commit)

Before clicking save, write a short title for your change, like "Adding 'The Great Gatsby' review". It's like writing your name in the sand—it lets the club know who shared what!
